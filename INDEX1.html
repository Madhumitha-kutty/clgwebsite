ABOUT US

K.S.R. College of Engineering, Tiruchengode is an autonomous college affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. It was started under Aarthi Educational and Charitable Trust in the year 2001 by our Founder & Chairman Dr. K. S. Rangasamy MJF., and Currently this institution is managed by the the younger son of our Founder & Chairman, Thiru R. Srinivasan BBM.,MISTE.,He act as the Chairman Cum Managing Trustee of this institution. The college is accredited by NAAC with A++ grade.
